# Human Authority Declaration

Hollow House Academy operates under explicit human authority.

Human Authority Holder:
- Organization: Hollow House Institute
- Steward: Human Authority (founder-controlled)
- Role: Final decision-maker, ethical arbiter, governance authority

AI systems used within this academy:
- Do not make final decisions
- Do not teach independently
- Do not override human judgment
- Do not personalize identity or psychological interpretation

All educational outputs require human oversight.
