# Safety Principles

Children are never isolated.
No diagnosis or psychological labeling.
Parents remain sovereign participants.
Any uncertainty triggers STOP.
# Safety & Child Protection

Hollow House Academy is designed for children and families.

Principles:
- No extraction of personal data
- No psychological manipulation
- No identity coercion
- No surveillance-based assessment
- No punitive or shame-based feedback

AI is used only as:
- reflective support
- pattern mirroring
- creative companion
- non-authoritative guide

Human authority always overrides AI output.
