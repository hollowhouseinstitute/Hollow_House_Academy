# Safety Framework

## Child Safety First
This academy is designed for children and families.

## Prohibited Content
- Sexual content or grooming
- Violence or self-harm
- Medical or mental health diagnosis
- Political persuasion
- Data collection from children

## Red Flags (STOP + HUMAN REVIEW)
- Shame-based discipline
- Isolation from caregivers
- Secret-keeping requests
- Identity coercion

## Incident Handling
All incidents must be logged in governance/audit/INCIDENT_LOG.md
